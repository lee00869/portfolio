Redesign Tututu Space Website

Problem

  • Some color contrasts are too low.
  • The navbar isn't responsive properly.
  • The service section and FAQ section are lack of design.
  • The reservation space should include some images.
  • The gift card page doesn't have any button or link to go back to the home page.
  • The images on the gift card page should be larger.

Solution

  • Ensure the color contrast is clear and visible.
  • Make sure the navbar is fully responsive.
  • Design a better layout for the service section and FAQ section.
  • Add images to the reservation page to enhance its visual appeal.
  • Add a navbar to the gift card page to allow easy navigation between pages.
  • Ensure the images on the gift card page are appropriately sized and visible.
