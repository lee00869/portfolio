Pain points

  • To address long wait times, include features for estimated wait times and order tracking.
  • To ease parking issues, add a feature showing nearby parking and crowd levels.
  • To avoid wrong orders, add a confirmation step and review option before checkout.
  • To combat high prices, offer discounts or a rewards program.
  • To highlight healthy items, include labels for healthy options.

Persona

User story

  • As a marketing specialist, I want to get my coffee and breakfast as fast as possible so I won't be late for the work.
  • As a software engineer, I want to have health food during work so that I can take care of my health without cooking.
  • As a college student, I want to have affordable coffee so I don't need to spend too much money on food.
